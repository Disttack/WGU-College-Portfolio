Welcome to Twin Falls

Twin Falls, known as "Niagara of the west", is an agricultural city at the intersection of several rivers and water falls. This city prides itself on having the oldest Native American artifacts in North America.

Fact Details
Population 54,300
Year Incorporated 1905
Region South Idaho
Classification Urban
Average Income 30,356 USD (State average is 39,000 USD)

Twin Falls gets it's name sake from the water fall of the same name that is divided by a basalt pillar. This city boasts the ramp that evel knievel used to jump the snake river.

Shoshone Falls

One of the largest water falls in North America, it is 45 feet taller than niagara falls. The park area offers many recreational services, including viewing the falls upclose on a kayak.
