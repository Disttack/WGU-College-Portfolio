Welcome to Coeur d'Alene

Coeur d'Alene is most known for its native american tribe, resorts, and lake front entertainment. With a unique blend of modern water entertainment and a history that predates colonialism, Coeur d'Alene is a perfect getaway.

Fact Details

Explore the lakefront, stay dry in the Farragut State Park, and experience the 5th deepest lake in the United States. Come see why Coeur d'Alene is truely an outdoor paradise.

The lakefront

The lakefront is comprised of 109 miles of usable shoreline, countless small shops and resturants, and the historic Coeur d'Alene Resort. Enjoy a boat tour or simply take a leisurely stroll along this iconic destination.
