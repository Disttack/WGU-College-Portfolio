Welcome to Pocatello

Pocatello, the "Gateway", is the entry city to idaho from the East. This town is defined by its historic importance to the oregon trail.

Fact Details
Population 57,730
Year Incorporated 1893
Region Southeast Idaho
Classification Urban
Average Income 29,037 USD (State average is 39,000 USD)

Immerse yourself in Pocatello's frontier history, learn about the unique buzz-saw sharks at the Museum of Natural History, and explore the other-wordly Craters of the Moon Nation Monument.

Fort Hall

Fort Hall was the early foundation of Pocatello. From 1834 to 1863 this outpost served the needs of the settlers and travelers entering the pacific northwest.
