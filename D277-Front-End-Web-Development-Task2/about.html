Mission Statement

Idaho Gov D277 is your premier online resource for information about the great state of Idaho. As your gateway to state governance, we aim to provide comprehensive and accurate details about the cities, culture, and unique features that make Idaho exceptional.

Fun fact!

Did you know that Idaho is called the "Gem State" because nearly every known gem has been found there? Idaho mines more than 72 different precious and semi-precious gems. The 43rd state has rich cultural, inventive and economic history. Explore more about the Gem State!
