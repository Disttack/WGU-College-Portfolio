Welcome to Boise

Boise, known as the "City of Trees," is the most populous city in Idaho. Boise is also the healthcare, argiculture, and technology capitol of the state.

Fact Details
Population 236,634
Year Incorporated 1864
Region Southwest Idaho
Classification Urban
Average Income 39,173 USD (State average is 39,000 USD)

Boise's skyline, cultural attractions, and microchip legacy make it a city like no other. Explore the Basque Museum and Cultural Center or enjoy the vibrant graffitti art that colors the city center.

Basque Museum

Discover the unique and wonderful history of the basque people and the role they played in settling the American West. Boise is home to the largest population Basque people outside of Europe.
